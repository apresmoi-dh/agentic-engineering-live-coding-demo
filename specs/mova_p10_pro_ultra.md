# MOVA P10 Pro Ultra

![MOVA P10 Pro Ultra](https://us.mova.tech/cdn/shop/files/0-2.jpg)
![MOVA P10 Pro Ultra features](https://us.mova.tech/cdn/shop/files/P10PU2_e531b20c-7e1b-4a81-904e-c51f6f288552.png)

The value pick. Full camera platform (video stream + speaker + mic) with Valetudo support at $399 — cheapest robot here that checks all the boxes. Likely shares Dreame platform DNA (same Fastboot + Breakout PCB root path).

## Why This One

- **$399** — less than 1/3 the price of Dreame X40 Ultra, with video stream + speaker + mic.
- **Valetudo via Fastboot** with Dreame Breakout PCB — identical root path to Dreame, suggesting shared platform.
- **Video call + two-way audio** — full remote presence for snapshots, pet interaction, home monitoring.
- **13,000 Pa suction** — solid mid-range cleaning power.
- **Dirt detection + auto re-mop** — self-corrects on dirty areas.
- **75-day dust bag** — matches the premium robots.
- **32 moisture levels** — same granularity as Dreame X40 Ultra.

## Watch Out

- Camera resolution unknown — likely lower than Dreame's 8MP or Eureka's FHD.
- Hardware internals undocumented — less community knowledge.
- 65°C mop wash (vs 70-80°C on pricier models).
- No water hookup, no cleaning solution dispense, no mop auto-removal.
