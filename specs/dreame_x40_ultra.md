# Dreame X40 Ultra

![Dreame X40 Ultra](https://global.dreametech.com/cdn/shop/files/x40-ultra-pc-group1_36_1600x.webp)
![Dreame X40 Ultra dock](https://global.dreametech.com/cdn/shop/files/x40-ultra-pc-group1_20_1600x.webp)
![Dreame X40 Ultra top view](https://global.dreametech.com/cdn/shop/files/x40-ultra-pc-group1_21_1600x.webp)

Dreame's flagship. Best-documented hardware for hacking — known SoC, RAM, flash, WiFi chip (RTL8189FTV). The 8MP front-facing camera (OV8856) is the highest resolution in this comparison, making it the strongest candidate as a mobile camera platform.

## Why This One

- **8MP camera** — best image quality for snapshots/surveillance use.
- **Best-known internals** — Allwinner MR813, 1GB RAM, 4GB eMMC. Community knows this hardware well.
- **Fastboot root** with Dreame Breakout PCB, all warranty seals intact.
- **Water hookup option** (sold separately) — can be plumbed for zero-maintenance water.
- **Mop auto-removal** — only robot here that drops its mops at the dock automatically.
- **Cleaning solution auto-dispense.**

## Watch Out

- $1,300 is steep — MOVA P10 Pro offers ~80% of features at $399.
- TriCut anti-tangle brush is a separate purchase.
- Runtime (~210 min) is estimated, not official.
