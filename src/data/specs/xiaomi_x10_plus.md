# Xiaomi Robot Vacuum X10+

![Xiaomi Robot Vacuum X10+](https://i01.appmifile.com/v1/MI_18455B3E4DA706226CF7535A58E875F0267/pms_1666877302.02075589.png)

Budget Valetudo-capable robot. The camera exists but is **navigation-only** — no video streaming, no speaker, no microphone. This rules it out as a camera platform.

## Why Not This One

- **No video stream, no speaker, no mic.** The camera is for obstacle avoidance only. Cannot be used for surveillance, snapshots, or two-way communication.
- 4,000 Pa suction — weakest by far.
- 120 min runtime — shortest.
- 7mm mop lift — lowest.
- No dirt detection, no re-mop.

## If You Just Need a Cheap Valetudo Cleaner

It's the cheapest locally-controllable robot here and does the basics fine: LiDAR nav, auto-empty, auto-mop-wash, multi-floor mapping. Just don't expect to take pictures with it.
